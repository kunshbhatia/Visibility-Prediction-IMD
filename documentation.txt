# INPUTS DOCUMENTATION FOR Output_Code.py

1) inputs_given :- Here are the inputs which we do need to give in the same format we are giving to the machine initially i.e Day , Month , Year , Time_hour , PM2.5 , PM10 , TMPC , DWPC , RELH , DRCT , SPED , BLH , VSBK_Current in form of a tuple

2) output :- Here we use "range" , "point" or "both" . When we use "range" , we get the range of visibility after adding and subtracting the RMSE from the visibility we predicted . When we use "point" , we get the point value of the visibility i.e. a single value of visibility . When we use "both" , we get both range and point value of visbility

3) units :- Here we will enter weather we need output in meters or kilometers using 'm' and 'km'

4) timeperiod :- Here we will enter in 3 ways :- (i) A single int number to get the prediction for T+n hour (ii) A tuple like (x,y) to get prediction between T+x and T+y (iii) "all" to get next 24 hours prediction

5) category :- It's either True or False , if True , then fog classification will be shown in the output table and visa versa when False

6) export :- Same either True Or False , if true then in csv format file would be downloaded , and visa versa when False.
